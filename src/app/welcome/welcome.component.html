About
______________

A platform that serves as a student guide for Barangay New Ilalim Daycare pupils. The CDLP App has many features in order to manage all aspects in a child development center, from students, to teachers, and and also for admins. It aims to help improve the children’s reading, writing and other learning comprehensions. This is to provide a channel for student-to-teacher exchange and communication. Also, it offers an infotainment learning for the children.
